About Us

Proper Chuffed Productions is an award-winning, Sheffield-based theatre company. We create energetic, socially relevant plays that champion northern voices and put women’s experiences centre stage. Since 2022, we've sold out runs in Sheffield, Leeds, London, and at the Edinburgh Fringe.

Upcoming Show

Top of the World by Fabien O’Farrell

Venue: Stephenson Theatre, Surgeons Hall (Venue 43) – EdFringe 2025
Dates: Friday 1st – Saturday 9th August
Time: 09:15 – 10:00 (Doors: 09:05 - 10:10)

Audrey has everything under control… nearly. Enter Evelyn, her no-nonsense, ‘perfect’ little sister. Two sisters, one fort, and a tupperware of bolognese.
A fast, funny dive into anxiety, sibling rivalry, and moving on.

Hilarious, heartfelt and deeply human, Top of the World is a quick-witted exploration of sisterhood, nostalgia and what it means to step outside your comfort zone.

Audience: 14+ (Contains distressing themes and strong language)

Bye Bye Baby by Fabien O’Farrell

Sold out at: Edinburgh Fringe, Sheffield, Leeds, London

A vibrant and fast-paced comedy set in a chip shop at the end of a night out, where three best friends drunkenly navigate a whirlwind of emotions and revelations about pregnancy, infertility, and their views on motherhood.
